Privacy Policy

REPD – Hangboard Timer & HIIT
Last updated: May 4, 2026

1. Overview

REPD is built to run entirely on your device. The app does not intentionally collect, store, or transmit personal data to the developer or third parties.

2. Local Data Storage

Workout settings, saved sessions, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data stays on your device and remains under your control.

3. Internet & External Services

REPD does not require an internet connection and does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Some operating system features may handle data independently of the app.

4. Data Sharing

No user data is shared with the developer or any third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

REPD does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to access, modify, or delete.
